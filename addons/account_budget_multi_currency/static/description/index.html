Managing Account Budgets with Multiple Currencies in Odoo

Optimizing Account Budgeting with Foreign Currencies in Odoo for View

This app enables your accounting or budget department to create a budget in a different currency.


Main Features:
  • Multi-Currency Budget Creation: Allows your team to create budgets in both company currency (Odoo standard) and other currencies.
  • Currency Selection Option: Choose whether to create the budget in company currency or another currency.
  • Budget in Company Currency: Option to select budgets in your company's default currency.
  • Budget in Other Currency: Option to select and create budgets in an alternate currency.
  • Display of Committed, Achieved, and Theoretical Amounts: Shows budget amounts for committed, achieved, and theoretical values in the selected currency.
  • Odoo Enterprise Compatibility: This module is compatible only with Odoo Enterprise Edition.
  • Visual Guides: Includes video tutorials and screenshots for easy setup and use.
  • For more details, please check the video and below screenshots.
  • Note: This module is compatible only with Odoo Enterprise Edition

The Budget Form in Odoo offers users the option to select the company currency, allowing them to create and manage budgets in the default company currency. This feature, part of the Odoo standard functionality, enables users to set budget amounts directly in the company currency, streamlining budget management and ensuring consistency across financial records.Note that the company currency budget is an inherent feature of Odoo and is available by default.

The Budget Form in Odoo, a standard feature, allows users to view and manage the committed, achieved, and theoretical amounts, all computed in the company currency. This ensures that the budget tracking reflects accurate financial data in the default currency of the company.

The Budget Form in Odoo, a standard feature, provides users with the option to select a currency other than the company currency for their budget. This allows users to set and manage budget amounts in a currency of their choice, offering flexibility for multi-currency budget planning.

The Budget Form in Odoo, a standard feature, allows users to view and manage the committed, achieved, and theoretical amounts, all computed in a currency other than the company currency. This feature provides flexibility for budget tracking and reporting in various currencies, ensuring accurate financial data for multi-currency budgeting.
